OCI Internship

Discovering the role of Systems and Networks Administrator in an IT Services Company.

Introduction

During my second year of the Networks and Telecommunications Bachelor's degree at IUT Colmar, specializing in Cybersecurity, I completed an 8-week internship (from April 20 to June 12) at the OCI agency in Mulhouse (68).

There, I discovered how a general IT service provider operates and was able to shadow field technicians, intervening directly at clients' sites for various projects and network issues.

Company Description

The OCI Group

The OCI group is a recognized IT Services Company (ESN), whose main mission is to support companies in the management, securing, and overall evolution of their information systems.

With over 1,300 employees, the group currently serves nearly 15,000 clients throughout France.

Services and expertise provided:

Infrastructure and Networks Cloud Cybersecurity Telecommunications Business Applications Web and digital

The Mulhouse Agency

The Mulhouse agency acts as a true local branch for OCI, located at 5 Av. de Strasbourg, 68350 Brunstatt-Didenheim. It allows the group to maintain a strong local presence.

Its clients mainly consist of SMEs located in the Haut-Rhin economic basin.

The agency's teams:

  • Systems and networks technicians and administrators
  • Sales engineers

Job Description & Completed Missions

Job Description

Systems and Networks Administrator

During this internship, I was in charge of shadowing field technicians. My missions covered a wide spectrum ranging from technical installations (on-site) to hotline support, including IT outsourcing missions.

Flagship Cyber Project

Complete implementation of an MFA solution: week of April 20 for Microsoft 365, and May 18 & 19 for the client VPN.

Details

Microsoft Environments

Configuration and audit of Cloud infrastructures (Microsoft 365, Entra ID, Intune).

Network Security

Configuration of VPN tunnels and setting up rules on physical WatchGuard firewalls.

Client Fleet Deployment

Physical configuration and installation of workstations, including the creation and management of user accounts.

On-site Interventions

Execution of half-day IT outsourcing missions directly within client companies.

Physical Infrastructures

Hardware installations and racking of servers and backup power systems (UPS).

Disaster Recovery

Management, supervision, and integrity verification of backups via the Veeam solution.

Wireless Connectivity

Physical deployment and logical configuration for the establishment of Wi-Fi networks.

Peripherals

Installation, network configuration, management, and repair of professional printers.

User Assistance

Various help and troubleshooting (hotline support, resolution of technical incident tickets).

Main Project

MFA Project - 365 & VPN

Deployment of a multi-factor authentication service within an SME

Microsoft 365 Entra ID WatchGuard Cybersecurity SAML
View Project

Conclusion

This internship was a particularly formative experience for me, as it gave me a clear vision of how an IT services company operates as well as a technical approach during the various interventions. I found the variety of topics covered particularly interesting.

Following this internship, I received a work-study offer at the Mulhouse agency for my 3rd year of the Bachelor's degree.
