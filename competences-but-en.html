UE1 - Administer

Administering Networks and the Internet

Building on the first-year UE1, this unit focuses on network infrastructure management and system administration, particularly with the basics of Active Directory. It adds a cybersecurity dimension, essential in the context of system and network administration.

Essential Components

  • CE1.01 Choose the appropriate network solutions and technologies.
  • CE1.02 Comply with the fundamental principles of IT security.
  • CE1.03 Use a rigorous approach to troubleshoot malfunctions.
  • CE1.04 Respect business rules.
  • CE1.05 Maintain technological watch.

Critical Learnings

  • AC21.01 Configure and troubleshoot dynamic routing in a network.
  • AC21.02 Configure and explain a simple QoS policy and basic network security functions.
  • AC21.03 Deploy client workstations and virtualized solutions adapted to a given situation.
  • AC21.04 Deploy advanced network services.
  • AC21.05 Identify carrier networks and Internet architecture.
  • AC21.06 Work in a team to develop professional skills.

Reflective Analysis

What I did well

During semester 3, I managed to understand and apply the new concepts in dynamic routing (MPLS, BGP) during the Multisite SAE, as well as in Active Directory (essential in business).

What I need to improve

Some practicals gave me trouble, particularly regarding the setup of Windows Server for example, but I managed to grasp the essential concepts.
